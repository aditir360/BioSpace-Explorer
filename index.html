What is BioSpace Explorer and how does it work?

BioSpace Explorer is your AI-powered gateway to NASA’s extensive space biology research. It transforms complex scientific publications and datasets into interactive visualizations, summaries, and reports. Whether you're a scientist generating new hypotheses, a manager identifying research priorities, or a mission architect planning safe exploration of the Moon and Mars, BioSpace Explorer helps you make informed decisions backed by NASA's cutting-edge bioscience data.

01 – Discover Space Biology Insights

We summarize each experiment’s goals, conditions, species studied, key results, and biological impacts—all in clear, accessible language. Users can compare studies side-by-side to find the most relevant research tailored to their needs, whether for hypothesis generation, investment, or mission planning.

Additional Details: Our AI-powered summarization intelligently extracts key scientific content from publications, saving users time and helping them quickly understand complex research findings.

Explore Insights

02 – Explore Data & Visualizations

Complex scientific information is broken down with interactive knowledge graphs, summarized text from Results and Conclusions sections, and data visualizations. Users can identify scientific progress, consensus, and knowledge gaps through multi-dimensional datasets collected from sources like the NASA Open Science Data Repository, GeneLab, and NASA Task Book.

Key Features: Interactive network visualizations, multi-modal data exploration, and intuitive search tools help users visualize relationships and deepen their understanding of space biology experiments.

Visualize Data

03 – Generate AI-Powered Research Reports

Users can input study information or select sets of experiments, and our AI organizes and summarizes this data into comprehensive, easy-to-understand reports. These reports describe research progress, identify knowledge gaps where additional research is needed, and provide actionable information. Users can download these AI-generated reports to support mission planning and scientific discovery.

Support Included: An integrated AI assistant guides users through report generation and exploration, answering questions and suggesting related studies to maximize research impact.

What is the issue we are trying to solve?

NASA’s space biology research produces extensive, complex data on how living systems respond to spaceflight conditions. However, this invaluable information is fragmented across many publications and datasets, making it difficult for scientists, managers, and mission planners to access, understand, and apply. BioSpace Explorer addresses this challenge by aggregating, summarizing, and visualizing space bioscience knowledge with AI-powered tools, enabling users to identify research progress, knowledge gaps, and actionable insights to support safe, efficient human exploration of the Moon, Mars, and beyond.
